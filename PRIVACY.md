# Privacy Policy

**StampIt - Digital Screen Stamper**

Last updated: March 29, 2026

## Data Collection

StampIt does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. All stamps are stored locally on your device in the Application Support directory. Nothing leaves your Mac.

## Screen Recording

StampIt requires Screen Recording permission to capture screen regions. Captured images are only stored locally and copied to your clipboard. They are never sent to any server.

## Analytics

StampIt does not include any analytics, tracking, or telemetry.

## Third Party Services

StampIt does not use any third party services.

## Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, open an issue at https://github.com/PauAbellaMolina/stampit
